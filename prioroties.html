PRIORITIES

PRIORITIES

KEEP ALPHARETTA’S MOMENTUM GOING

Alpharetta has become a destination city - our booming economy, safe neighborhoods, and top-tier amenities have made our city an aspirational place to live, work, and raise a family. Kirk will work with Council to continue the good work that has been done to make Alpharetta one of best cities in not just Georgia, but the entire country.

RESPONSIBLE, MEASURED GROWTH

Next year, Alpharetta will update its citizen-drafted land use plan. Residents and homeowners need assurance that their voices will be heard and that their wants and needs will come first. As a City Councilman, Kirk will be an advocate for reasonable development that puts the concerns of Alpharetta citizens first.

SAFE STREETS, STRONG NEIGHBORHOODS

Strong public safety is the foundation of any great city. Kirk will guarantee that our police and first responders have the resources and support they need and will encourage cooperation with our North Fulton neighbors to ensure that criminals can’t set up shop on our streets.

LOW TAXES, ACCOUNTABLE SPENDING

We need a responsible city government that exercises restraint with taxpayer dollars. Kirk is committed to keeping our property taxes low and making sure that Alpharetta remains fiscally responsible.
